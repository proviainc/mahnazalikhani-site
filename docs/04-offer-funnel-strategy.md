# Stage 04 - Offer & Funnel Strategy

## Primary Conversion Strategy

The website should optimize around one dominant action:

- Book an Event Strategy Consultation with Mahnaz.

CTA URL:

- `https://calendly.com/mahnazalikhani/event-strategy`

This action fits the personal-brand strategy because Mahnaz's value depends on context: event type, audience, stakeholders, budget, timing, risk points, guest experience, and operational complexity. A consultation is more appropriate than a generic contact form for high-stakes event, hospitality, and business-event opportunities.

## Secondary Conversion Strategy

Secondary CTA:

- Connect on LinkedIn.

Use LinkedIn for visitors who are not ready to book but want to verify credibility, follow Mahnaz, or start a lower-commitment professional connection.

Do not add many secondary actions at launch. Extra paths such as email, phone, ProVia Events inquiry, EventAgent demo, speaking inquiry, or newsletter should stay subordinate unless later approved as primary goals.

## Offer Packaging Recommendations

### Primary Offer

Name:

- Event Strategy Consultation

Purpose:

- Give qualified visitors a clear first step to discuss the event, audience, moving parts, risks, and next best path.

What it can cover:

- Event goals and audience priorities.
- Guest flow and arrival.
- Vendor and sponsor coordination.
- Timeline and program flow.
- Hospitality or service-flow concerns.
- Whether Mahnaz, ProVia Events, or another route is the right next step.

### Supporting Offer Frames

These should not become separate CTAs at launch, but can shape sections:

- Corporate & Business Event Strategy
- Luxury Private Event Strategy
- Hospitality Operations Consulting
- Speaking & Emcee Support
- EventAgent Strategy Context

## CTA Hierarchy

Primary CTA:

- Book an Event Strategy Consultation

Secondary CTA:

- Connect on LinkedIn

Contextual support CTAs:

- Visit ProVia Events, only where the user clearly needs the company-service site.
- Learn about EventAgent, only in the EventAgent context section.

CTA rules:

- The header should prioritize the Calendly CTA.
- LinkedIn can appear as a secondary header/footer link or near the bio.
- ProVia Events and EventAgent should not compete visually with the primary consultation CTA.
- Avoid generic "Contact" as the main label if Calendly is the desired action.

## Funnel Journeys

### High-Intent Buyer

Entry:

- Referral, LinkedIn, search for Mahnaz, ProVia Events validation, or direct domain.

Path:

1. Hero clarifies who Mahnaz is.
2. Proof strip reduces uncertainty.
3. Audience section confirms fit.
4. Selected experience builds confidence.
5. CTA routes to Calendly.

### Validation Visitor

Entry:

- ProVia Events prospect or EventAgent stakeholder checking credibility.

Path:

1. Hero confirms senior role and credibility.
2. About/experience sections explain background.
3. ProVia Events and EventAgent context sections clarify relationship.
4. Visitor either books consultation, visits the relevant brand site, or connects on LinkedIn.

### Early-Stage Visitor

Entry:

- Search or social visitor exploring event support.

Path:

1. Problem framing explains why event strategy matters.
2. "Where Mahnaz helps" shows use cases.
3. Proof and operating philosophy create trust.
4. CTA offers a clear first conversation, not a hard sales ask.

## Trust Architecture

Trust should be earned in this order:

1. Clear positioning.
2. Verified proof metrics.
3. Audience fit.
4. Selected experience.
5. Operating philosophy.
6. ProVia Events affiliation.
7. EventAgent strategy context.
8. LinkedIn verification.
9. Calendly CTA.

Do not rely on a portrait alone. Until more visual proof is available, proof metrics and specificity must carry the trust load.

## Friction Audit

Potential friction:

- Visitors may not know whether to book Mahnaz or contact ProVia Events.
- EventAgent could confuse the commercial path.
- The offer could feel broad if speaking, consulting, planning, and technology are presented equally.
- Limited visual assets could reduce perceived polish.
- Unapproved proof cannot be used yet.

Friction reduction:

- Keep "Book an Event Strategy Consultation" as the dominant action.
- Explain ProVia Events and EventAgent in supporting sections.
- Use one consultation path for most inquiries.
- Use proof numbers early.
- Add LinkedIn for trust and lower-commitment visitors.
- Avoid unapproved testimonials or claims.

## Conversion Components Needed

Launch-critical:

- Header CTA to Calendly.
- Hero CTA pair: Book Consultation + LinkedIn.
- Proof strip.
- Audience-fit section.
- Selected experience section.
- "What the consultation can cover" section.
- Footer CTA.
- External links to LinkedIn, ProVia Events, and EventAgent.

Not launch-critical:

- Native intake form.
- Newsletter.
- Downloadable guide.
- Blog/CMS.
- Separate speaking inquiry form.

## Recommended Booking Flow

Use Calendly at launch because the booking link already exists and matches the desired consultation path.

Future enhancement:

- Add a native pre-call intake form only if lead qualification becomes a problem or if Calendly alone produces too many low-fit bookings.

Suggested qualifying fields if a future form is added:

- Event type.
- Event date or estimated timing.
- Location/market.
- Guest count.
- Primary need.
- Budget range, if appropriate.
- Whether the inquiry is for Mahnaz personally, ProVia Events, EventAgent, or speaking/emcee.

## Analytics Events And KPI Recommendations

Primary KPI:

- Calendly booking clicks.

Secondary KPIs:

- LinkedIn clicks.
- ProVia Events outbound clicks.
- EventAgent outbound clicks.
- Scroll depth to selected experience.
- Clicks from hero versus footer CTA.

Quality indicators:

- Ratio of Calendly clicks to actual booked calls.
- Qualified call rate.
- Inquiry type distribution.
- Visitor path before booking.

- Offer structure supports audience needs and business value.
- Core user journeys are mapped.
- Funnel friction points are identified.
- Trust and proof requirements are clear.
