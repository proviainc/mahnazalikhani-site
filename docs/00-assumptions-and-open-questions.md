# Assumptions And Open Questions

## Assumptions

### Safe To Proceed

- The website repo and folder name use all lowercase: `mahnazalikhani-site`.
- The site is a new personal website, not a redesign of an existing MahnazAlikhani.com website.
- `MahnazAlikhani.com` is the intended production domain, but DNS and registrar access are not yet confirmed.
- The website should use English only.
- ProVia ownership, Cloudflare Pages, feature branches, preview deployments, and explicit production approval remain the default technical workflow unless overridden later.
- The primary conversion should be Calendly booking, supported by LinkedIn as a secondary trust and connection path.
- ProVia Events should be referenced as Mahnaz's operating company/context, not treated as the primary brand of this site.
- EventAgent should be referenced as a credibility and strategic-operations layer only after exact approved wording is verified.
- The site can begin with one portrait/photo and text-led credibility, then expand later with project images or case studies.

### Content Assumptions

- Mahnaz's strongest public positioning is the intersection of event strategy, live-event execution, hospitality operations, guest experience, and technology-informed coordination.
- Corporate/business events, luxury private events, hospitality consulting, and business-event organizers are commercially important enough to shape the first sitemap.
- Proof should emphasize specific event types, roles, attendee counts, vendor/sponsor coordination, hospitality consulting, and entrepreneurship rather than generic premium claims.
- Testimonials from the current ProVia Events website should not be used as final proof until approved and verified.

## Open Questions

### Blocking Before Final Copy

- What is Mahnaz's exact approved public title for EventAgent?
- Can we publicly state that she is "Head of Strategy" for EventAgent, or should the wording be softer?
- Which portrait/photo should be used, and what usage rights/format are available?

### Important Before Design

- Should the visual direction lean more personal-executive, premium event strategist, warm hospitality, or modern event-tech operator?
- Are there approved brand colors, typography, or a logo/wordmark for Mahnaz personally?
- Should the site visually connect to ProVia Events, EventAgent, both, or neither?

### Important Before Launch

- Who owns and manages `MahnazAlikhani.com` DNS?
- Should phone and email appear publicly on the website?
- Are there approved testimonials, logos, event photos, or press mentions?
- Are any privacy restrictions attached to private events, galas, or client names?
