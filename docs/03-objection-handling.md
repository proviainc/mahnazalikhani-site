# Stage 03 - Objection Handling

## Objection 1: Is This Just A Resume Site?

Concern:

- Visitors may assume the site is only a career profile.

Reframe:

- This is a personal authority and consultation site for clients, partners, and collaborators who want Mahnaz's event strategy and judgment.

Messaging response:

- Use commercial CTA language early.
- Show who she helps.
- Connect experience to event outcomes, not employment history.

## Objection 2: Should I Go To ProVia Events Instead?

Concern:

- ProVia Events already has a company website.

Reframe:

- ProVia Events is the company-service destination. Mahnaz's site is the personal credibility destination for visitors who want to understand the senior strategist behind the work.

Messaging response:

- Include a concise ProVia Events affiliation section.
- Avoid duplicating the ProVia Events service catalog.
- Keep the primary CTA as Mahnaz's consultation link.

## Objection 3: Is Mahnaz Too Broadly Positioned?

Concern:

- Event planning, hospitality consulting, emcee work, ProVia Events, and EventAgent could feel scattered.

Reframe:

- The common thread is structured event leadership.

Messaging response:

- Organize everything under one umbrella: event strategy, guest experience, and operational control.
- Treat speaking/emcee and EventAgent as supporting credibility unless later approved as primary paths.

## Objection 4: Does She Have Enough Proof For Corporate Or Luxury Buyers?

Concern:

- Buyers may need confidence before booking a consultation.

Reframe:

- Mahnaz has verified proof across large events, galas, corporate/business events, hospitality consulting, and business leadership.

Messaging response:

- Use numbers early: 10+ years, 18 years total experience, 100+ events, 800+ attendees, 750+ guest galas, 2,000+ hospitality-sector software clients.
- Keep proof factual and restrained.

## Objection 5: Is EventAgent Relevant Here?

Concern:

- EventAgent could confuse the site if it feels like a product pitch.

Reframe:

- EventAgent reinforces Mahnaz's event-systems perspective and strategy credibility.

Messaging response:

- Mention EventAgent in a contained credibility section.
- Explain the connection to structured coordination, vendors, timelines, contracts, and approvals.
- Do not lead with EventAgent unless the visitor is specifically in that context.

## Objection 6: Why Book A Consultation Instead Of Sending A General Inquiry?

Concern:

- Visitors may not know why Calendly is the main CTA.

Reframe:

- A strategy consultation fits the way high-stakes event needs are diagnosed: goals, audience, timing, stakeholders, risks, and moving parts.

Messaging response:

- Present the call as a focused first step.
- Explain what can be discussed.
- Keep LinkedIn as secondary for lower-commitment visitors.

## Objection 7: Is The Site Overclaiming?

Concern:

- The market is full of inflated claims around luxury, flawless execution, and unforgettable experiences.

Reframe:

- Mahnaz's credibility comes from specific experience and operational judgment.

Messaging response:

- Avoid "best," "flawless," and "world-class."
- Use specific proof and practical language.
- Hold unapproved testimonials, photos, and titles until verified.
