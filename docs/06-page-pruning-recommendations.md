# Stage 06 - Page Pruning Recommendations

## Launch Scope To Keep

Keep:

- Home.
- About.
- Experience.
- Event Strategy.

This is enough to support credibility, proof, conversion, and entity clarity without making the site feel like a thin agency clone.

## Sections To Keep

Homepage:

- Hero.
- Proof strip.
- Who Mahnaz helps.
- Operating philosophy.
- Selected experience.
- Event Strategy Consultation.
- ProVia Events and EventAgent context.
- Final CTA.

About:

- Bio/career arc.
- Approach.
- Credentials/proof.
- Context links.
- CTA.

Experience:

- Proof metrics.
- Event clusters.
- Selected examples.
- CTA.

Event Strategy:

- Problem framing.
- What the consultation covers.
- Best-fit scenarios.
- Preparation guidance.
- CTA.

## Sections To Avoid At Launch

Avoid:

- Generic services grid.
- Blog teaser without live content.
- Testimonials without approved testimonials.
- Logo strip without approved logos.
- Separate "Process" section if it repeats consultation coverage.
- Long FAQ unless real objections require it.
- Multiple CTA bands with different actions.
- Dedicated ProVia Events or EventAgent pages.

## Pages To Defer

Defer:

- Speaking & Emcee.
- Hospitality Consulting.
- Insights.
- Individual selected work pages.

Reason:

- These need more proof assets, clearer offer priority, or publishing commitment before they earn navigation space.

## Repetition Watchlist

Watch for repeated claims around:

- Structure.
- Calm.
- Event-day control.
- Guest experience.

Each repetition must add something new:

- Audience fit.
- Proof.
- Operational detail.
- CTA clarity.
- Relationship to ProVia Events/EventAgent.

## Pass Standard

Every section should answer at least one of these:

- Who is Mahnaz?
- Why trust her?
- Is this relevant to my event or business?
- What proof supports the claim?
- What should I do next?
