# Stage 01 - Discovery & Source Audit

## Project Overview

Mahnaz Alikhani's website is a new personal credibility site for a senior event planner, event strategist, hospitality consultant, and event-operations leader based in the Toronto area.

The site should help qualified visitors understand why Mahnaz is credible, where her expertise is strongest, how her work connects to ProVia Events and EventAgent, and how to book a consultation with her.

This is not intended to replace the ProVia Events website. It should act as Mahnaz's personal authority layer and a trust-building destination for people who want to validate her before booking, partnering, inviting her to speak/emcee, or trusting her strategy input.

## Business Objective

Primary objective:

- Build personal authority and generate qualified consultation bookings.

Supporting objectives:

- Strengthen credibility for corporate, luxury, hospitality, and business-event opportunities.
- Give ProVia Events prospects a personal trust page for Mahnaz.
- Support EventAgent credibility by connecting the product story to real event-execution experience.
- Create a polished destination for LinkedIn, referrals, speaking/emcee inquiries, and personal search.

## Audience Hierarchy

Primary audiences:

- Corporate and event clients
- Luxury private event clients
- Hospitality consulting clients
- Business event organizers

Secondary audiences:

- ProVia Events prospects validating the person behind the work
- EventAgent partners or early users validating Mahnaz's strategy and operations credibility
- Community, cultural, gala, and nonprofit event organizers
- Speaking or emcee opportunity contacts

Lower-priority or poor-fit audiences:

- Low-budget event buyers seeking only transactional staffing
- Visitors looking for a full ProVia Events company site
- Visitors seeking general career/resume information without commercial intent

## User Goals And Buying Triggers

Likely user goals:

- Verify Mahnaz's credibility before booking or referring her.
- Understand her experience across event types, event scale, hospitality, and business operations.
- See whether she is a fit for a high-stakes event where timing, guest flow, vendors, and room experience matter.
- Confirm that her work is structured, professional, and calm under live-event pressure.
- Learn how her event operations experience informs EventAgent.

Buying or engagement triggers:

- Upcoming corporate event, conference, gala, luxury private gathering, or business event.
- Need for an event strategy consultation.
- Anxiety about guest experience, vendor coordination, timeline control, or on-site execution.
- Need for someone who understands both hospitality and operational systems.
- Desire to work with a senior event lead rather than a generic planning service.

## Constraints And Risks

Strategic risks:

- Trying to serve too many goals equally could weaken the funnel.
- The site may duplicate ProVia Events if not kept personal and authority-led.
- EventAgent references could confuse the audience unless framed clearly.

Content risks:

- Limited initial visual assets.
- Some event names and private-event details may need discretion.
- Final claims must be tied to verified resume, LinkedIn, or approved business records.

Technical and launch risks:

- DNS ownership for `MahnazAlikhani.com` is not yet confirmed.
- GitHub/hosting setup is not yet confirmed, though ProVia and Cloudflare Pages are default assumptions.
- Production domain connection requires explicit approval.

## Strategic Assumptions

- The strongest site angle is "personal authority for structured event strategy and execution."
- The primary path should be consultation booking, not a multi-offer service catalog.
- ProVia Events should be used as a trust anchor and related company context.
- EventAgent should be used to show that Mahnaz's expertise extends into structured event systems, but final wording must be verified.
- LinkedIn should be visible in header/footer or authority sections as a trust asset.
