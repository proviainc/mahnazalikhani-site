# Stage 07 - Component Direction

## Navbar

Purpose:

- Orient visitors and keep the consultation CTA visible.

Direction:

- Left: Mahnaz Alikhani wordmark/name.
- Center/right: About, Experience, Event Strategy.
- Far right: Book Consultation CTA.

Rules:

- Desktop height around `76px`.
- Mobile height around `68px`.
- Logo/name max width `180px` desktop, `150px` mobile.
- CTA height `46px` desktop, `44px` mobile.
- No heavy shadow unless sticky state needs separation.

## Hero

Purpose:

- Establish positioning, trust, and next step quickly.

Direction:

- Editorial text block plus portrait/proof composition.
- H1 should carry the strongest hierarchy.
- Proof should appear in the first viewport or immediately after.

Rules:

- CTA group should include primary Calendly and secondary LinkedIn.
- Do not add more than two hero actions.
- Avoid decorative event stock imagery.

## Proof Strip

Purpose:

- Reduce uncertainty through verified numbers.

Direction:

- Horizontal row on desktop.
- Two-column or stacked layout on mobile.
- Numbers should be prominent; labels should be short.

Rules:

- No animated counters.
- No fake badges.
- No unapproved logos.

## Audience Cards

Purpose:

- Help priority visitors self-identify.

Cards:

- Corporate and business events.
- Luxury private gatherings.
- Hospitality consulting.
- Business event organizers.

Rules:

- Four cards maximum.
- Each card needs one concrete operational concern.
- Card padding should be consistent.
- Hover can lift subtly on pointer devices.

## Operating Philosophy Section

Purpose:

- Make the strategic differentiator visible.

Direction:

- Use a calm statement plus structured detail list.
- Consider two-column layout: principle on one side, operational details on the other.

Rules:

- Avoid dense paragraphs.
- Use alignment and spacing to express order.

## Selected Experience Cards

Purpose:

- Prove capability without needing a full case-study system.

Direction:

- Cards should include event name/type, scale, and role/responsibility.

Rules:

- Use privacy-safe information.
- Keep copy tight.
- Do not use images unless approved.

## CTA Bands

Purpose:

- Convert after trust has been established.

Direction:

- Compact editorial block with primary CTA.
- Optional secondary LinkedIn link.

Rules:

- No giant button.
- No generic "Get Started."
- Explain what the consultation is for when context is needed.

## Footer

Purpose:

- Close with composure and trust links.

Direction:

- Name/positioning.
- Primary pages.
- Trust/context links.
- CTA.

Rules:

- Keep dense enough to feel complete, not cluttered.
- Stack cleanly on mobile.
