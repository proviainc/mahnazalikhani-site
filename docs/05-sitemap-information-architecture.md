# Stage 05 - Sitemap & Information Architecture

## IA Principles

This website should be structured like a senior personal-brand site, not a full agency website. The sitemap should help visitors quickly understand:

- Who Mahnaz is.
- Why her judgment is credible.
- What kinds of event, hospitality, and strategy situations she can support.
- How ProVia Events and EventAgent relate to her personal credibility.
- How to book an Event Strategy Consultation.

The site should avoid navigation sprawl. Most conversion work should happen on the homepage, with a small set of supporting pages for deeper validation and search clarity.

## Primary Navigation

Recommended launch navigation:

- About
- Experience
- Event Strategy
- Book Consultation

Primary CTA button:

- Book Consultation

The header should not include ProVia Events or EventAgent as primary nav items. They are trust/context links, not main user journeys.

## Secondary Navigation

Footer/supporting links:

- LinkedIn

- Privacy, if needed at launch

- Insights

## Page Roles And Goals

### Home

Role:

- Main conversion and credibility page.

Goal:

- Establish Mahnaz's positioning, prove credibility, clarify audience fit, explain consultation value, and route visitors to Calendly.

### About

Role:

- Personal authority and story page.

Goal:

- Explain Mahnaz's career arc across hospitality, event planning, ProVia Events, Banic Software, and EventAgent context.

### Experience

Role:

- Proof and validation page.

Goal:

- Show selected events, event types, responsibilities, and proof metrics without becoming a portfolio-heavy agency page.

### Event Strategy

Role:

- Offer and consultation page.

Goal:

- Explain why the consultation exists, who it is for, what it can cover, and how to book.

## SEO And Discoverability Considerations

Launch SEO should prioritize entity clarity rather than a large keyword footprint.

Primary entity:

- Mahnaz Alikhani

Supporting entities:

- Event strategist
- Senior event planner
- Hospitality consultant
- ProVia Events
- EventAgent
- Toronto / GTA
- Event Strategy Consultation

Search visibility should support:

- Name search: "Mahnaz Alikhani"
- Role search: "event strategist Toronto"
- Offer search: "event strategy consultation"
- Adjacent credibility searches around ProVia Events and EventAgent.

Avoid overbuilding many SEO landing pages before content depth and proof assets exist.

- Key audience and offer journeys are supported.
