## Competitor Interchangeability Check

Weak:

- "Mahnaz creates unforgettable events with flawless attention to detail."

Stronger:

- "Mahnaz helps teams clarify guest flow, vendor roles, timeline pressure points, and program flow before event day."

Weak:

- "A trusted partner for luxury and corporate events."

Stronger:

- "Senior event strategy for rooms where guests, vendors, sponsors, and timelines all need to move in sync."

## Final Copy QA Questions

Before implementation, check every major section:

- Is the claim specific?
- Is the proof visible near the claim?
- Could this line appear on a competitor website unchanged?
- Does this section change belief, understanding, or action?
- Is there a clearer operational truth available?
