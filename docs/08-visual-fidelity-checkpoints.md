# Stage 08 - Visual Fidelity Checkpoints

## Global Direction

The built site must match the Stage 07 direction:

- Editorial Event Strategist.
- Senior, calm, warm, structured, and restrained.
- Proof-led, not decoration-led.

## Header

Pass:

- Header height near `76px` desktop and `68px` mobile.
- Name/wordmark does not overpower navigation.
- Book Consultation CTA is visible and proportionate.
- Mobile menu is simple and stable.

Fail:

- Oversized name/wordmark.
- Giant CTA button.
- ProVia Events or EventAgent in primary nav.
- Header feels like a generic template.

## Hero

Pass:

- H1 is the strongest visual element.
- Calendly CTA is visible early.
- LinkedIn is secondary.
- Portrait/proof area feels composed.
- Mobile first viewport communicates role and next step.

Fail:

- Vague headline.
- Stock/event imagery dominates.
- CTA is pushed too far down on mobile.
- Too many hero actions.

## Proof Strip

Pass:

- Verified metrics are easy to scan.
- No animated counters.
- Labels are concise.

Fail:

- Stats feel flashy or gamified.
- Unapproved claims appear.
- Metrics overflow or crowd on mobile.

## Cards

Pass:

- Audience cards are specific and limited to four.
- Experience cards include event type, scale, and responsibility.
- Padding, borders, radius, and hover states are consistent.

Fail:

- Cards read like generic services.
- Too many cards.
- Uneven spacing or random styles.

## CTA Bands

Pass:

- One dominant Calendly CTA.
- Copy explains the next step.
- Secondary LinkedIn link is visually subordinate.

Fail:

- New competing actions appear.
- CTA band is oversized.
- Generic labels like "Get Started" are used.

## Motion

Pass:

- Motion is minimal to moderate.
- Uses shared patterns.
- Reduced motion works.

Fail:

- Parallax.
- Looping decoration.
- Flashy counters.
- Random section animations.

## Responsive

Pass:

- No horizontal overflow.
- Cards stack cleanly.
- Tap targets are at least `44px`.
- Text widths remain readable.

Fail:

- Cramped mobile hero.
- Horizontal card rows.
- Tiny footer links.
- Cropped portrait harms first impression.

## Final Visual QA

Before merge:

- Review home page at mobile, tablet, desktop.
- Check About, Experience, and Event Strategy for hierarchy consistency.
- Confirm ProVia Events and EventAgent links are contextual.
- Confirm the site does not feel like a wedding planner template, corporate agency template, or SaaS landing page.
