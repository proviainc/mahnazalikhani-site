# Stage 09 - Content Placeholders

## Placeholder Policy

Placeholders may be used during implementation, but they must not ship unnoticed.

Public pages must not mention:

- Placeholder.
- Template.
- Starter.
- Future publishing.
- Internal workflow.
- Approval pending.
- SEO support.
- Production launch planning.

## Approved Temporary Handling

### Portrait

Use:

- Reserved portrait frame.
- Neutral abstract block.
- Initials treatment.

Do not use:

- Generic stock person photo.
- Event stock photo pretending to be Mahnaz.

### EventAgent Title

Use until confirmed:

- EventAgent strategy context.
- Work connected to structured event coordination.
- Event-technology strategy perspective.

Do not use until approved:

- Head of Strategy.
- Executive title claims.

### Testimonials / Logos

Do not render empty testimonial or logo sections.

Use instead:

- Verified proof metrics.
- Selected experience cards.
- LinkedIn link.

### Event Photos

Do not render image galleries until assets are approved.

Use instead:

- Text-first experience cards.
- Structured proof modules.

## Pre-Handoff Placeholder Check

Before implementation handoff:

- Search public app/content source for placeholder terms.
- Remove unused starter content.
- Ensure no empty sections are visible.
- Ensure no internal planning language appears on public pages.
